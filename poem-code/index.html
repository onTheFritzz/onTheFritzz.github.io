Follow a meandering career in tech...

Poem <=> Code

April 7, 2022 
"Cryptic riddles that attempt to both...
...solve problems and convey meaning."

It’s weird to come across a friend’s public projects to find that they’ve worked on nothing ‘fun.’
Like becoming acquaintances, just to learn they eat ice cream with their hands.

cup = []

creativity = 'passion'
profitable = 'passion'

while creativity == profitable:
  cup.append('<3')

Follow your passion. Do what you love. Your cup will overflow.
Follow a mission. Do what is interesting. You may find a buffer.
