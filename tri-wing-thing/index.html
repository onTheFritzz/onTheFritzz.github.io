Tri-Wing Ain’t No-Thing

July 12, 2022

Tags

  • Appliance Repair
  • Refurbish

What’s the point in coding without some Java? The big ol’ 14 cup, w/ a 4 hour long warmer function, Mr Coffee BVMC-PC14 stopped doing it’s thing. It would take forever to brew a pot and the kachunks could be heard from across the house. Unacceptable.

This is the first device taken part amongst thousands, that actually contained a tri wing screw. No problem. The iFixit kit has been waiting years for this moment.

Took it apart. Cleaned out the tubing (not a big deal) and the heating element. As assumed, the heating filament gathered some coffee grounds that the self cleaning function could not clear out.

Put coffee grounds into the filter while it is outside the pot! Lesson learned.

Just for good measure I took a pressurized garden hose to the tubing, elements and such. Put it back together, it’s brewing like you would expect.

Busted match busted.
