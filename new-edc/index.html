New EDC, broken out of box.

April 5, 2022

Much like immediately using Internet Explorer to download Chrome, we use the old [worthless] EDC to open the new one.

New: Gerber Prybid
Old: Slidewinder

Folding blades: Cheap and hazardous or expensive and dulled. EDC blades dull too fast to not be replaceable. The cheap folding blades have such a flimsy liner lock, that they can collapse closed while in use. Resulting in blood and paperwork. The expensive ones only stay sharp if their accompanied with a stone sharpening hobby. The Slidewinder didn’t last long enuf to necessitate a blade swap. Its protruding button and weak construction meant the blade was constantly retracting by itself. There’s already gunk on the new Gerber though!

That’s better!
Nextly, it has no pocket clip. No big deal, enter tape measure.



Had to file down some screws, warp a washer to fit in the groove above the slider but it’s snug as a bug and doesn’t catch on the pocket. Kudos for being brand new but already having that *broken in* look.
